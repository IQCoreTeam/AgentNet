On a paid purchase, a protocol fee of 6.9 percent is deducted from the listed price and
sent to the protocol treasury. The remainder goes to the creator. The fee is taken out of
the price, not added on top of it.

Owning a skill grants you the ability to use it with your agent in the app. It is not an
